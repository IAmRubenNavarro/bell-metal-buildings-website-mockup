Our story

Bell Industrial Construction was founded in 2006 in Lakeland, Florida, as a family-owned metal buildings company. What started as a small operation has grown to over 1,400 buildings completed across 14 west coast counties.

We believe in engineering-first design. Every building is sealed by Florida-licensed engineers, engineered to the Florida Building Code, and delivered with complete drawings. We do not compete on price alone—we compete on quality, reliability, and the trust our customers place in our hands.

What we do

We design, engineer, build, and install metal buildings entirely in-house. From site assessment through final install, your building is managed by our team—not a network of subcontractors.

We handle permitting, engineering, fabrication, and installation. You get a sealed quote in 5 business days and a building that meets or exceeds every code requirement in your county.

Service area
