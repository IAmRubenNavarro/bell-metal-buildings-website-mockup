Warranty

Guaranteed quality, backed by our written warranty. No asterisks.

Straight-wall warranty

Material, workmanship, and paint coverage for all straight-wall Bell buildings.

Material & workmanship

  • 50 years — Primary frame steel (columns & rafters). Covers manufacturing defects in heavy-gauge primary steel.
  • 40 years — Secondary frame (purlins & girts). Includes any pre-finish corrosion through the substrate.
  • 50 years — Rust perforation on Galvalume substrate. Panel will not perforate from atmospheric corrosion in 50 years.
  • 20 years — Roll-up doors. Hardware and finish through the manufacturer.
  • 10 years — Insulated wall and roof panels. Adhesion and thermal performance.
  • 1 year — Standard kit accessories. Vents, skylights, gutters, fasteners.

Paint warranty

  • 40 years — Premium SMP (silicone-modified polyester) finishes. Light to medium colors.
  • 30 years — Standard SMP finishes. Darker tones and metallics.
  • 25 years — Polyester finishes. Available on accessories and trim.
  • 10 years — Custom matched colors. Subject to mill-batch verification.
  • Fade and chalk — Bell will recoat or replace if fade exceeds 5 Hunter units or chalk exceeds rating 8 in the warranty period.

Arch-style (Quonset) warranty

Arch-roof buildings have a slightly different warranty profile because of the substrate.

Material & workmanship

  • 40 years — Galvanized arch panels. Primary structural skin.
  • 30 years — End-wall framing. Heavy-gauge cold-formed steel.
  • 15 years — Bolt-on accessories (doors, vents, windows). Through Bell or partner manufacturer.

Paint warranty

  • 30 years — Premium painted arch panels. SMP finishes.
  • 20 years — Standard painted arch panels.
  • Lifetime — Natural galvanized arch panels (no paint, exposed metal). As long as you own the building.

Warranty terms vary by climate, installation method, and maintenance. Full terms in the warranty document delivered with every kit. Questions? Contact warranty support →
