Why choose us

We engineer every kit. We don't just stamp them.

Most steel-building suppliers run a catalog of pre-engineered standard buildings and pick the closest-fit kit for your project. That's not engineering — that's order-picking.

Bell runs every quote through a real structural engineer who matches the kit to your zip code's snow, wind, and seismic loads, your foundation type, your accessory list, and your exposure category. The result is a kit that fits your project, not the catalog's average.

Quality controls

Strictly enforced manufacturing standards

Every coil we run is mill-tested before it touches our forming line. Every weld is inspected by a CWB-certified inspector. Every kit ships with a heat-number traceability sheet so you can verify the steel back to the foundry that poured it.

  • CWB-certified weld inspection on every primary frame component.
  • AWS D1.1-compliant fabrication process for structural welds.
  • UL-listed fire-rated wall and roof assemblies for commercial use.
  • FM Approved roof systems for high-wind and hurricane zones.
  • Independent third-party load testing on every standard frame profile.
Human-scale support

One name, one number, every call

When you order a Bell building, a real project manager is assigned to your account from quote through delivery. Same person. Same number. Their cell phone. We've gotten calls from customers at 9 PM on a Saturday asking which bolt goes where — and we've taken every one.

If we ever stop answering the phone, the customer is right to call someone else. We don't plan to ever let that happen.
