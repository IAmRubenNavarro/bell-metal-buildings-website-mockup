Open assumptions & flagged gaps

Every in this artifact (and every page that follows) marks something I'm guessing about. Confirm or correct any of these:

  • Brand name: using "Bell Metal Buildings Co." from the workspace folder. Confirm legal name / DBA / preferred capitalization.
  • Logo: placeholder is a single-letter "B" glyph + wordmark. Will substitute when a real logo is supplied.
  • Tagline: "Built once. Stands for generations." is invented as a placeholder. Replace with brief copy.
  • Contact info: phone, email, mailing address are dummy values.
  • Stats numbers: "40+ years", "65k+ buildings", "4.9★" are inherited from the inventory's structural pattern. Replace with the client's real stats — these must be truthful claims.
  • Product lineup: using 8 industry-standard categories (Garages, Workshops, Barndominiums, Quonset Huts, Carports, Container Covers, Commercial, Storage). Confirm which the client actually offers.
  • Pricing on clearance: all dollar values are placeholders.
  • Testimonials, customer logos, leadership name for "From the Desk of…": all placeholders.
  • Photography: CSS gradients stand in for hero/category/product photos. Will need a real photo library, or AI-generated original imagery, for handoff.
  • Geographic service area: US-only assumed (single-country footer/locations). Confirm.
