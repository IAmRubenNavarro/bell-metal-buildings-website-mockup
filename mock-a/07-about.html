Our story

Welcome to Bell Metal: 40 years of trusted excellence

Family-owned, factory-direct, and engineered like the buildings have to last.
M
From the desk of · Founder & CEO

"My father started this company with a rented stamping press and a handshake. Forty years later, we still answer the phone the same way he did."

When my father started Bell Metal Buildings, the steel building industry was a different place. Big distributors stamped out one-size-fits-all kits and walked away from the customer the moment the truck pulled out of the yard. He thought he could do better. He thought every kit should ship with engineering that matched the customer's site — not a generic load chart. He thought every customer should know the name and number of the person who'd answer if a beam went missing. He thought a 40-year warranty should mean 40 years, not 40 years with an asterisk.

Four decades later, every one of those promises is still on our quotes. We've shipped 65,000+ buildings across the country, sized from 12-foot single-bay carports to 200-foot clear-span warehouses. Every one came with stamped engineering, a real person on the phone, and a warranty document with no asterisks.

I run the company my father started. My daughter runs our quality program. The cycle keeps going because we believe the same thing my father believed: a steel building should be the best part of someone's investment, not the part they worry about.

If you're considering a Bell building, I'd love to hear about your project. My direct line is on the contact page.

—
, CEO · Bell Metal Buildings Co.

Quality control & standards

Bell engineers and manufactures to the same standards used by the largest commercial steel suppliers in North America.

  • CAN/CSA-A660 — Canadian Standards Association certification for steel building system manufacturers.
  • AWS D1.1 — American Welding Society structural welding code for primary frame fabrication.
  • UL listed for fire-rated wall and roof assemblies in commercial applications.
  • FM Approved roof systems for hurricane and wind uplift in high-wind zones.
  • AISC member — American Institute of Steel Construction certified fabricator program.
  • IBC compliance — All Bell engineering packages reference the latest International Building Code tables.
