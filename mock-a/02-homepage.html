Bell Metal Buildings FAQs

How long does it take to receive my kit after I order?
+
Most orders ship in 6–10 weeks from final engineering. Clearance kits in stock ship in 5–10 business days. Your project manager confirms a delivery window before drawings go to production.
Do I need a foundation in place before delivery?
+
Yes — your foundation should be poured and cured before steel arrives. We send anchor-bolt drawings as soon as engineering is locked so your slab work can run in parallel with manufacturing.
Will my kit be engineered to my local snow and wind code?
+
Every kit ships with stamped engineering matched to your zip code's snow, wind, and seismic loads. We pull from the latest IBC tables and adjust for local amendments.
Can I assemble the building myself?
+
Most owners with one or two helpers can put up our garage and workshop kits in a few weekends. Larger commercial spans benefit from a crew with a forklift or crane. We provide every bolt and panel labeled to the erection drawings.
What's included in a kit, and what isn't?
+
Included: primary frame, secondary frame, roof and wall panels, trim, fasteners, doors and windows you spec, sealants, and stamped drawings. Not included: foundation, anchor bolts, insulation (unless you add it), electrical, plumbing, permit fees.
Do you ship outside the contiguous US?
+
We ship to all 48 states. Alaska, Hawaii, and Canada are quoted case by case — talk to a rep about freight.
What's the warranty?
+
Primary frame: 50 years. Paint and finish: 25–40 years depending on color and panel system. Rust perforation: 50 years on Galvalume substrate. Full warranty terms at the warranty page.
Can I add insulation later?
+
Yes. We sell upgrade insulation packages that retrofit into existing Bell buildings — vapor-barrier blanket, reflective foil, or rigid panel systems.
Why don't you show prices on the website?
+
Steel pricing moves weekly and varies by code load, accessory mix, and freight. We post real engineered quotes within 24 hours instead of "starting at" numbers that won't match your build.
Can I change my design after I order?
+
Yes, before final engineering sign-off. Once drawings are stamped, changes trigger an engineering re-run and a possible freight delay — but we'll always quote the change before locking it.
Do you offer financing?
+
We work with several lenders on RTO and term financing for both residential and commercial buildings.
