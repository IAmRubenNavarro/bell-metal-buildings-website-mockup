About the company

Bell Metal Buildings was founded in 2006 in Lakeland, Florida, and remains family-owned. We serve agricultural, commercial, and residential builders across 14 west-coast counties with a product-engineering ethos: every building starts with site-specific engineering, sealed drawings, and Florida-approved components.

Our team of licensed engineers, steel fabricators, and installation crews works in-house. We don't outsource design, manufacturing, or assembly — which means faster turnarounds, tighter quality control, and direct accountability to our customers.
