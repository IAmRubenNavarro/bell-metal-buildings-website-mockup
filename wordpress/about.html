Our Story

Bell Industrial Construction was founded in 2006 in Lakeland, Florida by engineers and builders who were tired of cookie-cutter metal buildings and absentee companies. We built the company to answer a simple question: What if a metal buildings company actually understood Florida weather, Florida permitting, and the people who work the land?

Twenty years later, we have engineered and installed 1,400+ steel buildings across Citrus, Hernando, Pasco, Pinellas, Hillsborough, Polk, Manatee, Sarasota, Hardee, DeSoto, Charlotte, Lee, Collier, and Hendry counties. Every building has survived at least one hurricane cycle. Most have survived three.

We are still family-owned, still based in Lakeland, and still committed to engineering-first, honest-pricing steel buildings that work for Florida agriculture, agribusiness, commercial distribution, and light manufacturing.
