Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a steel building take to install?
Most buildings raise in 5-14 days once site preparation is complete and permits are pulled. Larger commercial buildings (60×100+) typically take the full two weeks; residential workshops and RV covers often go up in a week.
Do you handle the building permit?
Yes. We provide the sealed engineering drawings (signed by a Florida-licensed structural engineer) and submit the permit package to your county building department. You sign off on the application; we do the paperwork.
What wind rating will my building be engineered to?
Site-specific. The Florida Building Code assigns a wind speed by your county and exposure class. West coast inland counties (Polk, Hardee) typically design to 140-150 mph. Coastal counties (Lee, Collier, Sarasota) typically design to 160-170 mph. Your sealed drawings will show the exact value used.
What sizes can you build?
From 20×30 hobby workshops to 100×300+ commercial warehouses. Width is generally constrained between 20 and 100 feet for clear-span; length can run as long as the site allows. Eave heights from 8 ft (small storage) to 24 ft (commercial / equestrian arenas).
Do you offer financing?
Yes — through our financing partners. Options include traditional installment financing for residential and commercial buyers. Approval is typically returned in 24-48 hours. We can include financing pre-qualification with your initial quote.
What's included in the price?
The price includes the building kit (frame, panels, fasteners, trim), sealed engineering drawings, delivery to your site, and installation by our crew. Slab/foundation, site preparation, electrical, and interior finishes are quoted separately based on your needs.
