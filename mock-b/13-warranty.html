What's covered

Bell Metal carries a tiered warranty across the four major component groups of a steel building: the structural frame, the roof and wall panels, the factory paint finish, and the accessory hardware (doors, windows, vents, fasteners). Each tier has its own coverage window and its own claim procedure.

Straight-wall warranty profile

Material & workmanship

50 years
  • Primary red-iron frameRust-perforation, structural integrity, weld joints. Covered against factory defects.
  • Secondary cold-formed membersGalvanized purlins, girts, eave struts.
  • Galvalume roof & wall panels26-gauge PBR or R-panel. Coverage starts at the panel core, before paint.
  • Sealants & closuresManufacturer-supplied sealants.

Paint & finish

40 years
  • Factory paint on roof & wall panelsAgainst chalk, fade, peel, blister. Pro-rated after year 20.
  • Factory paint on standard trimEave, gable, base, corner trim — matches panel coverage.
  • 14 standard finish colorsCustom colors covered with the same terms, subject to factory match.

Painted accessories

25 years
  • Roll-up, sliding, bifold, walk doorsFactory paint and frame.
  • Window framesFactory-finished surfaces.
  • Cupolas, vents, skylightsFactory paint and seal.

Fasteners & erection

20 years
  • Bell-supplied screws and boltsStainless or zinc-coated. Against failure under design loads.
  • Erection-period defectsOne-year period from delivery — Bell will replace any panel that arrives misfabricated.

Arch (Quonset) warranty profile

Material & workmanship

40 years
  • Arch panels (S, P, Q models)Cold-formed galvanized steel. Rust-perforation and shape integrity.
  • End-wall framesIf purchased with the kit. Otherwise covered under straight-wall terms.
  • Sealants & closuresMatches straight-wall terms.

Paint & finish

35 years
  • Factory paint on arch panelsAgainst chalk, fade, peel, blister. Slightly shorter than straight-wall due to curved-panel coating physics.
  • End-wall panel finishMatches straight-wall terms.

Exclusions and edge cases

Standard exclusions: damage from acts of God beyond the design loads we engineered to; chemical attack from agricultural runoff or industrial fumes (covered with the optional marine/agricultural coating); cosmetic dents from hail (covered separately by the homeowner's insurance, typically); modifications made by anyone other than Bell to the structural frame after delivery.

How to file a claim

Call us. The fastest path is a phone call — we'll send a Bell engineer or a recommended local inspector to confirm the issue, and we'll route the warranty claim through the right factory queue. There is no online claim form because we'd rather a human eyes-on your situation than a checkbox.

Service line

The Bell service line is open for the life of the building. If you have a question 15 years from now — about a replacement part, a re-paint, a re-roof, an addition — we're still here.
