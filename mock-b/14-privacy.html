Bell Metal Buildings Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy.

This Privacy Policy describes how Bell Metal Buildings Co. ("Bell," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information when you visit our website, request a quote, or otherwise interact with us. By using the site or submitting information through the Get-My-Price flow, you agree to the practices described below.

1.Our secure system protects you

Bell Metal uses industry-standard encryption (HTTPS for all pages, TLS 1.2+ for form submissions) and limits access to personally identifying information to employees with a business need to know. We do not store payment card information on our systems; financing transactions are handled by our financing partners and are subject to their own privacy practices.

  • All form data is encrypted in transit and at rest.
  • Access to project records is role-based.
  • Personally identifying information is retained no longer than necessary for the quote and build process.

2.Information we collect

When you request a quote or contact us, we collect information you provide voluntarily, including your name, email address, phone number, mailing address, the type of building you're interested in, and any project details you choose to share. We also collect technical information automatically (IP address, browser type, pages viewed) for analytics and fraud prevention.

3.How we use information

We use the information you provide to prepare your quote, fulfill your order, follow up with you about your project, and improve our products and services. We do not sell your information to third parties. We may share limited information with service providers (CRM, email, shipping) as needed to deliver your building.

4.Marketing communications

If you opt in, we may send build tips, clearance alerts, and product news. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link at the bottom of every email. Required transactional emails (order confirmation, shipping notices, warranty documents) are sent regardless of marketing preferences.

5.Cookies and tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies for session management, analytics, and (with your consent) marketing measurement. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings or our cookie banner. We respect Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals where required.

6.Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or port the personal information we hold about you, or to object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact privacy@bellmetal.example. We will respond within the timeframe required by law (typically 30–45 days).

7.Children's privacy

Bell Metal's services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

8.Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced via email to active customers and via a banner on the website. The "last updated" date at the bottom of this page reflects the most recent revision.

9.Contact

For privacy questions, contact privacy@bellmetal.example or write to Bell Metal Buildings Co., 1234 Forge Road, Suite 200, Denver, CO 80112.
