Why steel

Steel Building vs. Traditional Construction

Bell Metal building system

  • Engineered for your county's loads
  • Fixed-price quote — no change orders
  • Bolt-together — self-erect or pro crew
  • 50-yr rust / 40-yr paint warranty
  • Clear-span interior up to 200′
  • Project manager stays with you

Conventional stick-built

  • Site-cut framing — weather delays
  • T&M pricing — change orders pile up
  • Load-bearing interior walls limit span
  • Often no manufacturer warranty
  • Wood frame — termite, rot, fire vulnerability
  • No factory engineering review
