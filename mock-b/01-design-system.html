How this file is used

01 — Color

Palette tokens

Anthracite Indigo grounds the page. Ember Orange is the only saturated CTA hue (used sparingly so it stays loud). Conduit Blue is the info / "learn / browse" voice. Gold for trust accents (stars, verified badge). Burgundy reserved for urgency strips so it never competes with Ember.

14 — Arrow-bullet callout

"What determines my price?"

Sits inside True Pricing (Site-2 "Rising Steel Prices" pattern). Numbered bullets with a circle-arrow numeral.

What determines my price

  1. 1

    Building size & clear-span

    Wider/longer clear-spans need heavier primary framing, which drives the steel-by-weight cost.

  2. 2

    Snow, wind & seismic loads

    Your county's loads drive the engineering. A high-wind coastal site costs more than an inland low-snow site.

  3. 3

    Doors, windows & openings

    Every opening needs reinforced framing. Two roll-ups cost more than one — but cheaper than adding one later.

  4. 4

    Insulation & finish package

    R-value, wainscot, color, trim, and ridge vent options all live here.

  5. 5

    Current steel market

    We hold your quote price for 30 days; after that the global hot-rolled-coil index resets it.

Comparison split (homepage)

Bell Metal building system

  • Engineered for your county's loads
