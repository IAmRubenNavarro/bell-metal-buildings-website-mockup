Steel Carports for Every Use Case

A Bell Metal carport isn't a wood-frame structure with metal cladding bolted on top — it's a pre-engineered, fully sealed, bolt-together kit designed for your county's snow, wind, and seismic loads from the first panel. That means a 30×40 carport in Pueblo gets different framing than a 30×40 in Bozeman, and you don't have to do the engineering math yourself.

Our carport line covers the four most-asked-for footprints: single-car (12×20), two-car (24×24), three-car (30×30), and four-car (40×40), plus any custom width up to 80′ in 5′ increments. Every kit ships flat on one truck with sealed drawings, an anchor-bolt plan, an erection manual, and a dedicated project manager who stays with your build from quote to delivery.

You can self-erect with friends in a long weekend, hire a local crew, or ask us to recommend an erection contractor working in your region. Either way, your engineering, your warranty, and your service line stay with Bell.

What's Included In The Kit

Primary red-iron frame and secondary cold-formed framing, 26-gauge Galvalume-coated PBR roof and wall panels (14 standard colors), trim, fasteners, sealants, anchor-bolt template, and full sealed structural drawings. Doors and windows are spec'd at quote time so the openings come pre-cut — not site-cut after the panels are on. Optional add-ons: wainscot, overhangs, lean-tos, insulation packages, cupolas, walk doors, sliding doors, bifold doors, roll-up doors, gable vents, ridge vents.

Warranty & Service

Bell carports carry a 50-year rust-perforation warranty on Galvalume panels, a 40-year paint warranty on factory finish, and a 25-year warranty on painted accessories. Every Bell building is backed by a service line for the life of the kit — if a panel ever fails to spec, you're talking to a human in our engineering office, not a phone tree.
