Our advantage

What's in a Bell kit

Five standard features, two popular add-ons, and seven categories of construction where pre-engineered Bell kits make the most sense.

The Bell kit

A complete pre-engineered building, in one delivery.

Bell ships every kit as a single coordinated package: primary frame, secondary framing, panels, trim, fasteners, full assembly drawings, and the stamped engineering. No drop-shipped components from third parties. No "we'll send the trim separately."

Standard features

Five things every Bell kit ships with

PBR roof panels

1¼″ major rib with 9″ on-center pattern. Standard 26-gauge, 24-gauge available.

Silicone-polyester coating

40-year limited paint warranty. Resists chalking, fading, and corrosion in 14 colors.

Galvanized girts & purlins

Hot-dip galvanized secondary framing. Pre-punched in Aliquippa to match your kit exactly.

Stainless-capped fasteners

Self-drilling roof and trim fasteners with stainless caps to prevent bleed-staining.

I-beam main frame

Hot-rolled, welded in-house, stamped by a P.E. licensed in your state.

Popular add-ons

Beyond the standard kit

Standing-seam roofs

Premium concealed-fastener roofing for buildings with a higher curb-appeal bar — commercial shells, barndominiums, road-facing garages. 18 colors, 50-year paint warranty.

Solar-ready roof packages

Where Bell kits work hardest

Seven categories of pre-engineered construction

01

Retail showrooms

Storefront-grade shells with stamped facades, large openings, and trim packages that read commercial from the curb.

02

Garages & auto-repair shops

High-eave bays, ventilation, lift clearance, and oil-tight floor transitions — engineered around how the work actually moves.

03

Industrial & manufacturing

Clear-spans up to 120 ft. Crane-rail engineering on request. Heavy floor-load reinforcement at primary frame footings.

04

Agriculture

Vented eaves, sliding-door packages, deep-snow roof loads, and corrosion ratings for the chemistry inside a working barn.

05

Recreation

Riding arenas, indoor sport halls, ATV-storage clubs — long clear-spans with viewing galleries and warming offices.

06

Faith & worship spaces

Bell has stamped sanctuaries, fellowship halls, and outbuildings for congregations of every size. We don't apologize for the cost-per-pew math.

07

Public & municipal

Salt sheds, equipment garages, recycling-transfer pads — engineered to public-bid spec, with documentation an inspector wants.
