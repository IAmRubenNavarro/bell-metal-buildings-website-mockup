Simple & efficient construction

Erect a Bell mini-storage in days, not months.

Bell mini-storage kits ship with pre-cut frames, pre-drilled panels, pre-matched fasteners, and assembly drawings stamped for your jurisdiction. Your contractor (or one of our recommended crews) puts the shell up in days — not weeks.

Every kit includes a direct phone line back to the engineer who stamped your plans. Contact us to walk through your build.
