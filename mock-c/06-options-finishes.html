Specifications

Options & finishes

Every detail is engineered to exceed your expectations. Components, coatings, fasteners, and color — annotated below.

Feature 01

PBR roof panels

Bell's purlin-bearing-rib profile gives a 1¼-inch major rib for clean fastener seating and a 9-inch on-center pattern that sheds water cleanly. Standard 26-gauge galvanized; 24-gauge available on request for high-load regions.

Feature 02

Silicone-polyester coating

A high-flex silicone-polyester top coat applied over a galvanized substrate gives Bell panels a 40-year limited paint warranty. Resists chalking, fading, and corrosion in coastal, snowbelt, and high-UV conditions.

Feature 03

Galvanized girts, purlins & C-sections

Every secondary framing member — girts, purlins, eave struts, base channels — ships hot-dip galvanized for life-of-building corrosion protection. Pre-punched in our Aliquippa fabrication line to match your kit exactly.

Feature 04

Stainless-steel-capped self-drilling fasteners

Visible roof and trim fasteners ship with stainless-steel caps to prevent the bleed-staining that plain steel screws cause over time. Self-drilling tips speed assembly without pre-drilling pilot holes.

Feature 05

I-beam main frame

Every Bell building is built around a hot-rolled I-beam primary frame, engineered for your wind and snow load. Welded in-house in Aliquippa and stamped by a P.E. licensed in your state before it ships.
