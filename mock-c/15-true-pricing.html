The Bell quote

A single sealed number with everything in it.

Bell's quote is the all-in factory-direct cost of your building kit, delivered to your site address. There's no separate engineering fee, no shop-drawing fee, no delivery surcharge unless your site requires a special-permit load. We list every component on the quote so you can match it line-by-line against any competitor.

The two things almost no kit-supplier includes are your foundation and your erection labor, and we don't either. Concrete and crew rates vary too widely by region for a Pennsylvania factory to honestly quote — but we'll connect you to vetted local crews who've put up Bell kits before.
