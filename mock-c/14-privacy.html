01. Who we are

Bell Metal Buildings Co. ("Bell," "we," "our") is a Pennsylvania corporation with its principal place of business at 2200 Furnace Road, Aliquippa, PA 15001. This privacy policy applies to information collected through bellmetalbuildings.example and any subdomains, by phone, and by any printed form a Bell representative gives you.

02. What we collect

We collect three categories of information:

  • Information you give us directly — name, email, phone, mailing address, building size and use, intended project state, any text you enter into our quote or contact forms.
  • Information collected automatically — IP address, device type, browser, referring URL, pages viewed, time on page. Standard web-server log data and Google Analytics 4 events.
  • Information from third parties — if you reach us through a referral partner, the partner may provide your name and project details. We don't buy contact data from data brokers.

03. Why we collect it

We collect this information to:

  • Prepare and deliver your quote, engineering, and building.
  • Respond to questions you ask through the contact form, phone, or email.
  • Send the Field Journal newsletter, if you've opted in.
  • Understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it.
  • Comply with legal obligations (tax, accounting, regulatory).

04. How long we keep it

Project records (quotes, drawings, delivery documents) are kept indefinitely — Bell warrants buildings for up to 50 years, and we need the engineering record on file to honor that warranty. Marketing contact information is retained until you unsubscribe. Web analytics data is retained for 14 months under our GA4 configuration.

05. Sharing & disclosure

We share information with: (a) our processors and contractors who help us run the business (CRM, email provider, shipping carriers, P.E. engineering partners in states where Bell doesn't hold a license); (b) legal authorities when required by law; (c) successor entities in the event of a merger or sale. We do not sell your personal information.

06. Cookies & tracking

The site uses session cookies for basic functionality, persistent cookies to remember your form progress, and Google Analytics 4 cookies for traffic measurement. We do not use cross-site advertising cookies. You can clear cookies in your browser at any time without losing access to the site.

07. Your rights

Depending on your state, you may have the right to: (a) request a copy of the personal information Bell holds about you; (b) ask us to correct it; (c) ask us to delete it, subject to our project-records retention obligations; (d) opt out of marketing communications. To exercise these rights, email us at privacy@bellmetalbuildings.example or write to the Aliquippa address above.

08. Security

Bell stores customer data in encrypted form at rest, with access limited to authorized employees and contractors on a need-to-know basis. We have not had a publicly-disclosed data breach. If we ever do, we will notify affected customers within 30 days of discovery, as required by applicable state law.
