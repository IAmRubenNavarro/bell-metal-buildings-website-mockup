Type scale, line height, tracking

Display + headline: line-height: 1.06, tracking 0.005em. Body: line-height: 1.65. Eyebrow: tracking 0.22em, uppercase. Fraunces variable axes: opsz 9–144, SOFT 0–100. Larger displays use higher SOFT for warmth.

03 · Foundations

Spacing, radius, shadow

4px-base spacing scale (s-1 = 4px through s-11 = 160px). Radius is restrained — small for inputs, medium for cards, no chips beyond the search/pill primary. Shadow is subtle; the editorial direction relies on borders and rules more than elevation.

99 · Notes

Assumptions & open questions for client

Carrying assumptions through from the plan-approval step. Each todo chip on the build pages corresponds to one of these.

  • brand Logo: single-letter "B" inside a brass-spot spruce square is a placeholder mark — needs a real Bell wordmark / glyph from the client brand assets.
  • copy Tagline: "Built once. Stands for generations." is invented. Confirm or replace.
  • phone Phone & address: 1-800-444-2525 and 2200 Furnace Road, Aliquippa PA 15001 are placeholders.
  • photos Imagery: all photos rendered as CSS gradient stand-ins. Production needs ~80–100 real Bell photos (hero, 12 use cases, projects, factory, founder, before/after, blog).
  • history Founding year: "Since 1962" and "64 years" are working assumptions. Confirm real founding year + founder name.
  • products Use-case set: 12 categories will mirror site 3's count — Garages, Workshops, Storage, Commercial, Industrial, Barns & Farming, Aircraft Hangars, Warehouses, Horse Arenas, Mini-Storage, Barndominiums, Sheds. Confirm Bell actually offers each.
  • colors Color matrix: 13 panel + 6 trim are placeholder values. Need Bell's actual SKU swatches and names.
  • warranty Warranty durations: 40-year paint and rust-through are modeled on industry norms. Confirm Bell's actual warranty document.
  • testimonials Testimonial copy & initials: placeholder. Need permissioned customer quotes.
  • stats 42k buildings, 48 states, A+ BBB: placeholder claims. Replace with defensible numbers.
  • script Pattern motif: chose repeating brass-dot halftone for the footer instead of copying site 3's Statue of Liberty silhouettes. Flag if you want a different motif.
