Simple & efficient construction

A kit that goes up like a dovetail joint.

Bell buildings ship as a complete pre-engineered kit: pre-cut frames, pre-drilled panels, pre-matched fasteners, pre-stamped drawings for your jurisdiction. Your contractor (or our recommended crew) erects the shell in days, not months. No mid-build redesigns. No "we forgot to ship that piece."

Every kit includes step-by-step assembly drawings and a direct phone line back to the engineer who stamped your plans. Contact us to walk through your build.
