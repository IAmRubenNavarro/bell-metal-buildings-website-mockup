Our Story

Bell Metal Buildings began in 1987 with a single crew and a conviction that metal buildings deserved the same precision and care as any other construction discipline. In an industry often characterized by speed over quality, we chose a different path.

We do not simply erect buildings -- we engineer solutions that protect our clients' investments for decades to come.

Over nearly four decades, that commitment has shaped every aspect of our operation. We invested early in engineering capabilities, building an in-house team that could design structures optimized for each client's specific requirements rather than relying on generic catalog plans.

Today, we operate from a 60,000 square foot fabrication facility with the capacity to produce custom components for the most demanding projects. Our field crews are OSHA-certified and trained in the latest erection techniques, and our project managers maintain direct communication with every client from contract through completion.

The result is a company built on repeat business and referrals -- clients who have seen firsthand that our buildings stand the test of time, and that our word is as reliable as our steel.
