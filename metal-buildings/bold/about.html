Our Story

We started with a single welding rig and a conviction that metal buildings did not have to be disposable. Every structure we fabricate is engineered for decades of service -- not just code minimums.

Our team combines old-school steel craftsmanship with modern pre-engineering software, delivering buildings that go up fast without cutting corners on quality. From a 2,400 sq ft hobby shop to a 100,000 sq ft distribution center, we treat every project with the same precision.

We fabricate in-house, which means tighter tolerances, faster turnaround, and direct accountability. When your name is on the steel, you make sure it is right.
