Our Story

When my father started this company, he did it with a welding rig, a flatbed trailer, and a handshake. There was no office, no sales team, no glossy brochure. Just a man who knew steel and a community that needed buildings. He raised barns for neighbors, shops for small businesses, and warehouses for anyone who asked. He did it right the first time, every time.

That was thirty years ago. Today we have a proper yard, a crew of skilled ironworkers, and the equipment to tackle buildings ten times the size of those early barns. But the principle has not changed: we build with our hands, we stand behind our work, and we treat every project like our name is on the line -- because it is.

If you need a metal building, we would like to hear about it. Come by the shop, give us a call, or fill out the form below. We will walk your site, talk through your needs, and give you an honest number. No pressure, no games. That is how my father did it, and that is how we still do it today.

Our Promise

  • Honest Pricing, No Surprises We quote what it costs. If something changes, you hear about it before we proceed.
  • Built to Last, Not to Impress Every building is engineered for your climate, your loads, and your use case -- not for a photograph.
  • We Finish What We Start Our crew stays on your job until the last trim piece is fastened and the site is clean.
