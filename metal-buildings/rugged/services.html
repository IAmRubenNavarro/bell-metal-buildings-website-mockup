What We Offer

Pre-Engineered Buildings

Factory-designed, field-assembled structures that cut lead times without compromising strength. Ideal for offices, retail, and light manufacturing facilities.

Dimensions Up to 200' clear span
Materials Galvalume steel panels
Lead Time 8 - 12 weeks
Foundation Concrete slab / pier

Steel Warehouses

High-clearance storage and distribution buildings engineered for heavy loads, crane systems, and maximum interior flexibility. Built to handle the weight of real work.

Dimensions Up to 300' x 150'
Materials Hot-rolled structural steel
Lead Time 12 - 18 weeks
Eave Height 24' - 40'

Barns & Agricultural

Purpose-built agricultural structures for livestock, equipment storage, and crop processing. Ventilated, insulated, and designed for the demands of working land.

Dimensions 40' - 120' wide
Materials 26-gauge steel cladding
Lead Time 6 - 10 weeks
Options Ventilation, insulation

Workshops & Garages

Personal and commercial shop buildings with wide-span doors, reinforced floors, and wiring-ready frames. A proper place to wrench, weld, or tinker.

Dimensions 24' - 80' wide
Materials Steel frame & panel
Lead Time 4 - 8 weeks
Doors Roll-up to 16' wide

Custom Fabrication

When off-the-shelf does not cut it, we design and fabricate steel components to your exact specifications. Canopies, mezzanines, structural modifications, and beyond.

Scope Fully custom
Materials Structural & misc steel
Lead Time Varies by project
Includes Engineering & drawings
