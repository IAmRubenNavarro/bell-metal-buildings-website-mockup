Get to know us

Twenty years of building steel — and never selling something we wouldn't put on our own farm.

Bell Metal Buildings was founded in Lake City, Florida by Kevin Bell, a farmer's son who grew tired of barn quotes that doubled at install. Today we run our own factory, our own install crews, and our own pricing — no third parties, no markups, no surprises.

Our vision

Make steel buildings as honest as the people who buy them.

Customers shouldn't need a contractor's license to read a building quote. We publish our pricing, our gauges, and our warranty in plain English — and we build to it.

Deliver on the price we quoted — every time.

No surprise delivery fees. No anchor upcharges. No "you didn't tell us about the slope" letters. The price in your inbox is the price on your slab.
