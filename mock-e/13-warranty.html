Coverage tiers

  • Galvalume panels — 20 yearsWarranted against rust-through perforation under normal atmospheric conditions.
  • Frame steel — 20 yearsWarranted against deterioration. Coastal upgrades available for 35-year coverage.
  • Paint finish — 40 yearsColor-coated panel warranty covers fading and chalking thresholds defined by ASTM standards.
  • Doors & hardware — 1 yearManufacturer warranty pass-through; we coordinate any claim directly.
  • Workmanship — 1 yearAnchor sets, panel seams, trim joints. We come back and fix it.

Claim process

Email or call

Send photos to warranty@bellmetalbuildings.com or call us direct.

We review

Most claims are decided in 5 business days. Coastal/storm claims may take longer.

We schedule

If approved, a Bell crew is on-site within 30 days for repair or replacement.

Resolution

Repair, replace, or refund. Your choice if the building can't be made whole.

What's not covered

Acts of God beyond what your engineering spec was designed for. Modifications by third parties. Failure to anchor per stamped drawings. Normal wear (door seals, fasteners, paint touch-up).

Need to file a claim?

We answer warranty calls in under 4 hours during business hours.
