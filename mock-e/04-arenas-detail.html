Common questions

Arena FAQ

Standard mid-size arenas install in one day. Larger buildings (40×60+) typically take 2–3 days. Our install crew sets ground anchors, raises the frame, sets the panels, and hangs the doors — all included in the price.
Galvalume panels are warrantied 20 years against rust-through. With proper anchoring and routine touch-up, expect 40–60 years of service life. Our 12-gauge frame is warrantied for 20 years against deterioration.
Every dimension is customizable — width, length, wall height, door count, window count, and color. Use the 3D Designer to configure exactly what you need and see the price in real time.
Most jurisdictions require a building permit. We provide stamped engineered drawings with every order at no extra cost — your local permit office can issue from those drawings directly.
