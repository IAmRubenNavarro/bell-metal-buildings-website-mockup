Our Story

Bell Metal Buildings was forged in the field -- not in a boardroom. We started with a single crew, a welder, and the conviction that industrial construction should be done right the first time.

Twenty-five years later, we have built over 2 million square feet of industrial space across the Southeast. Our crews are the backbone of every project -- experienced, certified, and relentless about quality.

We do not subcontract our core trades. When you hire Bell, you get Bell people on your site, with Bell standards, delivering Bell results.
