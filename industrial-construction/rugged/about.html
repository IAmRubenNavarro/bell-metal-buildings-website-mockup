Our Story

Bell Industrial Construction started with a handshake, a flatbed truck, and a crew of five in 1987. Our founder, Harold Bell, had spent twenty years working steel on rigs and refineries across the Gulf Coast before deciding he could do it better on his own. He was right. That first year we completed three small jobs and earned a reputation for showing up early, working clean, and finishing on time.

Through the 1990s we grew steadily, adding concrete and foundation capabilities and expanding into full-facility construction. We never chased growth for its own sake. Every new service line came from a client asking us to do more because they trusted the work we had already done.

Today we operate across the southern United States with a workforce of over two hundred skilled tradespeople. Our equipment yard holds cranes, excavators, and concrete pumps that we own outright -- no rental markups, no scheduling delays. The company is still family-led, and the values have not changed: honest work, fair pricing, and buildings that last.
