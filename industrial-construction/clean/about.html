Our Story

Bell Metal Buildings was founded in 1987 with a singular conviction: that industrial construction demands the same precision and care as the most exacting architectural work. What began as a small structural steel crew in South Florida has grown into a full-service industrial construction firm trusted by manufacturers, logistics operators, and energy companies across the Southeast.

Over three decades, we have erected more than four million square feet of industrial space. Every beam, every pour, every connection reflects our belief that the structures powering American industry deserve to be built with uncompromising standards.

We do not build buildings. We build the foundation upon which entire industries operate, and that responsibility shapes every decision we make.

Our team brings together licensed structural engineers, certified welders, experienced project managers, and skilled tradespeople who share a common commitment to safety, precision, and client partnership. We approach each project as a collaboration, working closely with owners, architects, and engineers to deliver facilities that perform flawlessly for decades.

Today, Bell Metal Buildings remains family-owned and independently operated. That independence allows us to prioritize long-term relationships over short-term margins, and to invest in the people and processes that produce exceptional work.
