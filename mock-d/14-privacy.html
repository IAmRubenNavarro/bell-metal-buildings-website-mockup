1. What we collect

Information you give us (name, email, phone, ZIP, message) when you request a quote, sign up for financing, or submit a contact form. Information collected automatically (IP, browser, pages viewed, referring URL) by our analytics tooling.

2. How we use it

To answer your inquiry, schedule a delivery, process payment, send order updates, and (with your opt-in) share occasional product news. We do not sell your contact info.

3. Who we share with

Financing partners (when you request financing), shipping carriers (when you order), and analytics providers. Each operates under a written data-processing agreement.

4. Your rights

You can request a copy, correction, or deletion of your data at any time by emailing privacy@bellmetalbuildings.com. California and EU residents have additional rights under CCPA and GDPR; we honor them regardless of where you live.

5. Cookies

We use essential cookies (cart, login state) and analytics cookies (Google Analytics). You can disable non-essential cookies in your browser; the site will keep working.

6. SMS & email consent

If you opt-in to SMS quotes, you'll receive transactional messages tied to your quote. Reply STOP to opt out anytime. Standard message rates may apply.

7. Changes

If we materially change this policy, we'll post the change here and email everyone with an active quote.
