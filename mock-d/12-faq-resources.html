Frequently asked

Everything you might ask before you buy.

No accordions. No fluff. Skim the whole page — Ctrl-F is faster than clicking 20 chevrons.

What payment options do you accept?

Credit card, ACH, cashier's check, financing (0% intro APR options available), and rent-to-own. Apply for financing in 90 seconds online.

What roof pitches are available?

3:12 standard; we can go up to 6:12 on most buildings. Steeper pitches require engineering review. Snow-load zones may require steeper pitches by code.

Can I attach the building to my existing home?

Yes, with a lean-to attachment. We design a flashed transition that ties to your existing roof line. Your local code may require a separation.

Is insulation available?

Single-bubble, double-bubble, and rigid foam packages. See the Options & Finishes page for R-values by climate zone.

Can I add on later?

Most buildings can be expanded. We engineer extension brackets that bolt to your existing frame. Plan for it during the original order if you can — it's cheaper.

Do you need a level pad?

Within 4 inches across the longest dimension. We can install on a slope, but anchor design changes and price changes too. Send a photo and we'll quote your specific site.

What anchors are used?

Concrete: wedge anchors. Dirt or gravel: 30" mobile-home anchors or 36" rebar. Coastal: engineered helical anchors. We include the right anchors in every quote.

What roof options are available?

Vertical roof (panels run top to bottom, our most popular), A-frame (horizontal panels with peaked gable), regular roof (rounded corners, lowest cost). See the roof selector on any building page.

How long do delivery and installation take?

Standard buildings: 2–6 weeks from order to install. Custom builds: 4–8 weeks. Installation itself: 1 day for buildings under 30×40, 2–3 days for larger.

Is financing actually available?

Yes — three lenders, ranging from 0% intro APR (with credit) to rent-to-own (no credit check). Apply online; decisions in 60 seconds.
